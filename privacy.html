03
Lead Generation & the EONS AI Tool

This section specifically addresses our lead generation activities and how the EONS AI tool captures and processes prospect information, as these are core business operations of EONS Animation Studio.

How Lead Data Is Captured

When a visitor accesses the EONS AI tool, they are required to provide their name, email address, and optionally their company name before the tool becomes active. This constitutes an explicit, affirmative act of data provision.

  • Lead data (name, email, company, timestamp, conversation type) is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key eons_leads. It is not automatically transmitted to an external server at the moment of capture.
  • When you submit a concept via "Send to Studio," your lead data and conversation transcript are compiled and transmitted to EONS via our EmailJS integration and stored as a lead record.
  • When you submit a contact form inquiry, your data is transmitted via EmailJS to our studio inbox and recorded internally as a lead.
  • EONS may use lead contact information to follow up regarding your project inquiry, schedule a strategy call, or send a response to your submitted concept. We do not enroll you in automated marketing sequences without your explicit consent.
AI Conversation Data
  • Your AI conversation messages are transmitted from your browser to our Cloudflare Worker proxy server (eons-api-proxy.ryanruichen03.workers.dev), which then forwards them to the Anthropic Claude API.
  • Cloudflare Workers process requests ephemerally — messages are not stored by the proxy beyond the duration of the API call.
  • Anthropic may process conversation data per their own Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review it at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Conversation history is stored only in your browser's localStorage until you clear it. It does not leave your device unless you choose to submit a concept to our team.
  • If you submit a concept, the full transcript is included in the submission and received by our team via email. This transcript is retained for business purposes as described in Section 8.
  • You may clear your AI conversation history at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for eons.studio, or using the clear function within the tool.

Business purpose of lead generation: EONS Animation Studio is a boutique studio that uses the AI tool as a qualifying and educational resource to help potential clients develop project briefs before engaging our team. Lead data is used exclusively for client outreach, project scoping, and business development. We do not resell, rent, or license lead data to any third party.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data under the following legal bases as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable UK data protection laws:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — Where you have actively provided information, submitted a form, or accepted cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Contract Performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — Where processing is necessary to fulfill a contract with you or take pre-contractual steps at your request (e.g., responding to a project inquiry or delivering animation services).
  • Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR) — Where processing is required to comply with applicable law, such as financial record-keeping, tax obligations, or responding to lawful requests from authorities.
  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — Where we have a legitimate business interest that is not overridden by your data protection rights, such as operating and improving our website, securing our infrastructure, or following up on a project inquiry you initiated.

For processing based on legitimate interests, we have assessed that our business interests are proportionate and do not override your fundamental rights, particularly given the nature of our services and the limited, purpose-specific scope of data collection.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

  • Contact form submissions and AI concept submissions — Retained in our internal records for up to 3 years from the date of submission, or until you request deletion. This retention supports business development tracking and potential future engagement.
  • Active client project data — Retained for the duration of the project plus 7 years to comply with New York State business record-keeping requirements and applicable tax law (IRC §6001).
  • Invoices and financial records — Retained for a minimum of 7 years as required by federal and state tax regulations.
  • Analytics data — Google Analytics data is retained per our Google Analytics retention settings, currently set to 26 months.
  • AI conversation data (localStorage) — Stored locally in your browser with no server-side expiration. You control deletion by clearing localStorage.
  • Email correspondence — Retained in our business email system for as long as the business relationship is active and up to 3 years thereafter, unless deletion is requested.
  • Server/CDN access logs — Retained for 30–90 days for security and infrastructure purposes, then automatically deleted.

When data is no longer required for its stated purpose, we will delete or anonymize it in a commercially reasonable timeframe. To request earlier deletion, see Section 10.

Data Security

EONS Animation Studio implements reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • Our website is served over HTTPS/TLS (enforced via Cloudflare), encrypting data in transit between your browser and our servers.
  • API communications between the EONS AI tool and Anthropic are transmitted over encrypted connections via our Cloudflare Worker proxy.
  • Dashboard access is protected by a SHA-256 hashed password with rate limiting, lockout after repeated failed attempts, and session token protection against replay attacks.
  • AI conversation data is stored client-side in browser localStorage, meaning it does not transit our servers unless you explicitly submit a concept.
  • We limit access to personal data within our organization to personnel who require it to perform their role.
  • We do not store payment card data — all payment processing is handled by PCI-compliant third-party processors.

Important limitation: No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take data security seriously and apply reasonable safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

If you discover a potential security vulnerability affecting our services, please report it to eonstudioanimation@gmail.com.
